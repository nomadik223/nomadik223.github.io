Wondering what Pint Defiance offers? Here is a little bit from their website! Looking to find craft and specialty beers in Tacoma? Pint Defiance is your source for great beer in Tacoma, University Place and Fircrest. Pint Defiance offers a wide variety of specialty, hard to find beers in the bottle and on tap. With 16 cooler doors, you have 50 feet of beers, meads and ciders to explore.

Here we have an excerp from their website, detailing why the owner opened the establishment: "After trying my first Guinness, I found there were other flavors and complexities within each variety of beer that domestic beers didn't offer. As time went on my palate expanded and I leaned more towards darker and malty styles. After graduating in Industrial Design, I moved to Portland Oregon to try and further my design career. I started experimenting and creating my own fine beers from home. After the urging from a friend, I moved to Tacoma and am able to bring my passion to you. Join me at The Copper Door and if you have a favorite beer you'd like me to try or carry, I would love to hear about it. Or just stop by and I will assist you in finding that perfect beer for any occasion or no occasion at all, just relaxation and enjoyment."
